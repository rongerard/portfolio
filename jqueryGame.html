Project Description

"Sort a Element" is an interactive sorting game that leverages the drag and drop functionality of jQuery UI. Players are challenged to categorize elements from the periodic table into their respective groups with precision and accuracy. For instance, Helium (He) must be strategically placed within the Noble Gases category. Our game is meticulously crafted with the aim of transforming the exploration of the periodic table into an engaging and immersive experience. By seamlessly blending entertainment with educational content, we endeavor to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation for the intricacies of chemical elements.
