The length of this wired access point is 40 meters, but I bought 50 meters in case of emergencies. The cable used is CAT-5 with a straight wiring type. Despite this, it still worked, likely due to the router's Auto MDI-X support. I encountered an issue accessing the router’s admin page due to a specific login requirement, but online resources helped resolve it. The most time-consuming task was lining the cable in the attic, which has a complex structure. After a week, a connectivity issue arose due to mice chewing the cable, requiring repairs and preventive measures.
