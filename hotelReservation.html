Project Description

This project, built during my second year studying BSIT, was developed using Microsoft Visual Studio and C#. It made me realize that our lack of experience in collaboration posed a significant challenge.At this stage, we don’t have a clear plan for the project, but we still assigned tasks to each other in an effort to move forward but we encountered several issues, particularly with the tight two-week deadline our professor gave us. Although we tried to collaborate, I ended up doing most of the work, as my team created a front-end that relied on a complicated plugin. Given the time constraints, I simplified the design to make it more manageable, but didn’t have enough time to fully apply all the changes. Despite not going as planned, this was a valuable learning experience, as it was my first time working on a application project with a team. It taught me the importance of clear communication, realistic planning, effective delegation, and adaptability, all of which are crucial for successful teamwork and time management in future projects.
