Project Description

This project marks my first experience developing a game in Unity. I handled all the coding and sourced the sprites from the internet. With the help of my group, we designed the characters, bringing the game to life. Labyrinth of Nightmares is a survival adventure game that follows Alexander, or Alex, a 3rd-year IT student. Alex is an average student but has an immersive imagination. One day, while exploring a construction area in his university, he falls into a mysterious void. Now, he must fight his way back to reality.
